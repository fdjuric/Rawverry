Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really handmade?

Yes! All pieces obtainable here are 100% handmade and unique, carefully crafted with toxin-free oil and acrylic paints on high quality cotton canvas.

Do you invidualize upon my wish?

Your wish is our command - any input or special wishes? We will try our best to create the very masterpiece that you desire.

How long til I receive my artwork?

It depends on your exact location, but we usually ship in less than 7 business days. Of course we will provide you with a tracking link once your artwork is on it's way to you.

What happens after i submit my order?

Upon receiving your order, our team of artists will create your desired artpiece. Once completed, we will send you photos and a video of your artwork. If you seek any changes of whatever kind, we will redo it and stay in touch with you. Once you are satisified we send the artwork to you and provide you with a tracking link.

What happens if I am not satisfied?

Not satisfied? Free redo or full reimbursement! If for whatever reason you can not be satisfied, we offer you a full reimbursement(including your shipping costs).
