Delivery Conditions and Shipping

Delivery costs may be added up to the product price, if so, you can see them at any time in the logging-out process. If not stated otherwise next to the product or in the check-out process, the delivery to your country of residence is free, which is also shown during the check-out process. After receiving your order we take up to 3 days to process your order, once processed you will receive a tracking link for your product/s. Delivery times depend on your state of residence but will take up to about 14 days. In case it takes a way larger amount of time for your state of residence you will get informed about that during the check-out process.
